Whats On?

Writers Group
Biblocafe hosts a writers group once a month – themes, times and dates are updated in the shop window but if you would like more details just Contact Us

Exhibitions
Every month, from the 1st to the very last, a different artist will exhibit their work in the mezzanine. Should you wish to find out who’s in - or book a slot for yourself – simply Contact Us.

Reading Group
Biblocafe hosts a reading group every month – titles are displayed in the shop window so if the choice tickles your fancy pick up a copy, form an opinion and - love it or hate it - come along! Should you not be passing just Contact Us for this month’s book.

Spanish Lessons
Maria is a Spanish native and qualified teacher with experience at High School, College and University who is now holding regular classes at Biblocafe. Please contact spanishteacher55@gmail.com for further details or to discuss tailored requirements.

Reservations
The mezzanine at the Biblocafe can be reserved for book clubs, reading groups or other meetings. We can seat 14 people comfortably and are happy to pre-arrange refreshments.
