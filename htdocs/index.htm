Biblocafe is an independent coffeehouse and secondhand bookshop & swap in Glasgow’s West End.

Created with love and nurtured with caffeine, from the beginning Biblo was intended to be more than just a place to sit out the rain – we wanted to create a warm atmosphere, serve superb coffee and, of course, recommend a good book.

Since March 2006 we have watched friendships develop, dissertations written and a community of book-lovers gather.

We buy from local suppliers wherever possible and aim to be flexible and adaptable to our customer’s needs whilst maintaining the integrity of our concept:

Second-hand books, First rate coffee, Seven days a week.
