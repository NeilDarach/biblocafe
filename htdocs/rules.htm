Bookswap Rules

Bring a book to the Biblocafe and, based on its condition, a price will be offered.

All titles and genres will be considered.

A credit note will be given to the value offered but no cash under any circumstances will be paid.

Credit notes can be used in full or part payment for any books in stock.

There is no expiry date to the credit notes (at least until our bank manager notices this oversight - he has very strong opinions on unlimited credit) but lost notes will not be replaced

Credit notes may not be redeemed against any other goods.

You don't have to accept our offer - these are second hand books we're talking about after all, and unless you find a wealthy collector of water-damaged Faye Kellerman's happy to pay a small fortune for a battered copy of Street Dreams, you are unlikely to be retiring on the proceeds any time soon. Just smile, say "No, thank you" and put it back on your bookshelf!
